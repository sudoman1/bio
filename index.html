About Sevier Academy

For over 20 years, Sevier Academy has been committed to providing outstanding education that prepares students for success in an ever-changing world. Our beautiful campus along the Sevier offers a serene learning environment where students can focus on academic excellence while developing critical thinking skills, creativity, and character.

Academic Excellence

Advanced Placement Program

Over 20 AP courses available, giving students the opportunity to earn college credit while still in high school. Our AP pass rates consistently exceed national averages.

STEM Innovation Lab

State-of-the-art laboratory facilities for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Students engage in hands-on learning and real-world problem solving.

Arts & Humanities

Comprehensive programs in visual arts, music, theater, and literature. Our students regularly win state and national competitions in creative fields.

Admissions Information

We welcome students who are eager to learn and grow in our supportive academic community. Our rolling admissions process makes it easy to apply throughout the year.

Application Process

Submit your application online with transcripts, recommendations, and a personal essay. We also encourage campus visits to meet our faculty and students.

Financial Aid

Need-based financial assistance is available. We believe every qualified student should have access to an excellent education regardless of financial circumstances.

Campus Tours

Schedule a personalized campus tour to see our facilities, meet teachers, and experience the Sevier Academy difference firsthand.
